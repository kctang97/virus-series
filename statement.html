Artist Statement

My art practice explores new media while incorporating the knowledge and skills I learned from the university.

During the process of making my digital art pieces, I began to realize my goals as a digital artist.

I like to explore the things that I commonly pay less attention to, like the projects I have done for Art 75 and 74 as a beginner.

However, I create works based on curiosity and interest in a medium and how it affects others differently.

My inspirations are composed of flashes I see in my imagination.

It may come from the news, events that I experienced, or the emotion of a memory.

But, I want to create something personal with my perspective of the world. Because I never know I would be a digital artist one day,

so I am producing much of my projects while exploring the varieties of digital mediums such as Adobe series Photoshop, Premiere,

After Effect, Illustrator, and a 3D modeling software Blender.

Therefore, the projects I had done in 2019 - 2020, implement the personal elements and events that I experienced in my projects.

They are my concept of art and mostly build on top of the concepts that arise in my brain.

The contents collect from the observation from my surrounding environment.

I continue to develop my digital style with input from class critiques and lessons from classes.

The goal of my projects is mostly to experiment with different media that I am not familiar with and use them to express my emotional world and my ideology.

This is a capture of an Augmented Reality project I made using the iPhone.

Animation 345, 1920 x 1080, Digtal Animation, 3 min 45 sec, 2020

The animation is a series I am creating specifically for the period we live in now.

In this series, I am using both After Effect and Photoshop for the entire series due to my artist practice.

The content of this is to explore the emotion and reaction of people from everyday news reports I seem on the television and network.

Also, the perspective of people like me viewing this health crisis.

The reason I called it Animation 345 is because I want the length of this animation to be the actual day,

which is 345 days. Pretend when a disaster like the animation shows, or the crisis we live in now.

The duration of the crisis can end quickly, like the length of this video 345 days.

If countries from all over the world work together, accept each other's efforts in solving the crisis.

The crisis now and the crisis in the future will be less pain, less depressing,

and less stressful for everyone compare to what happened up to this period.
